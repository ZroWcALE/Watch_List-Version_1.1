There is a 1985 animated superhero film called The Secret of the Sword, also known as He-Man and She-Ra: The Secret of the Sword. It is a compilation of the first five episodes of the TV series She-Ra: Princess of Power with minor edits made. The film is part of the same continuity as the He-Man and the Masters of the Universe cartoon series and was created by the same Filmation production team and cast. The movie follows the story of Princess Adora, who discovers she is the long-lost twin sister of He-Man, Prince Adam. She-Ra joins forces with He-Man to stop the evil Horde from taking over the planet Etheria. Additionally, there is a He-Man and She-Ra: A Christmas Special TV movie from 1985, where He-Man and She-Ra team up to stop Horde Prime from ruining Christmas for some Earth children stranded on Eternia
